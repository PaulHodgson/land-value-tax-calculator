If you're on a low income, you may be entitled to some help towards paying your Land Value Tax. This is called Land Value Tax Reduction (LVTR).

To apply for LVTR you need to meet the following criteria

  • You must live at the location where you are applying for LVTR. (You cannot make a claim for a reduction on Commercial land or a second residential location)
  • If you are temporarily away from home and of pensionable age. The government will allow you to apply for LVTR provided your period of absence does not exceed 12 weeks
  • Your income must not exceed the upper limit of £25000 per year. (This includes income from Employment, state or private pension). Your rate of LVTR will be calculated based on your actual level of income.
  • Your savings must not exceed the capital limits of £16000

Your Land Value Tax notification form will include an application form for LVTR. If you choose to receive a paperless notification a link will be included so you can apply online.
